UNC Baseball Roster

The UNC Baseball Roster was built using Python with the Django framework. I focused on usability and the ease at which the roster could be read. Often times, rosters for sports teams are clunky and hard to read. My goal was to recreate a roster that is clean and readable. There were many challenges I had to overcome when creating the application. I had to decide what information to add when there was none available. The freshmen on the baseball team do not have a summary of previous years or images on file. I had to design the application in a way that would indicate information would be forthcoming as soon as their first season ended. The absense of images left pages feeling blank. That is one of the many reasons I included Twitter feeds to each of the player's pages. This application is currently in the deployment phase. I hope to have the project deployed soon.
