Homefront Magazine Website

A magazine class in UNC's School of Journalism published a magazine for iPad and print for families of the military. I took the content to create a website for the magazine. My first task was to categorize the type of content in the magazine logically to create a website that would be easy to navigate. There were three main types of stories based on location of those in the military: when they are on base, deployed and when the families are at home. I used WordPress and Twitter Bootstrap to create a responsive, content-driven design.
