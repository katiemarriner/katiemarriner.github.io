Mental Health Treatment Facilities in North Carolina

This data visualization was created using Highcharts, Tabletop.js with GoogleMaps and DataTables with data from datahub.io. It was important that I let the data tell the story. North Carolina has been in the news recently for its lack of mental health facilities, as is the rest of the United States. The state is divided into three regions, the Piedmont holds the Research Triange Park and the largest city of Charlotte. Outside of the piedmont region, to the east is the eastern region and to the west, the mountain region. Both are predominantly rural. The American Psychological Association (APA) released a statement about the need for psychologists in rural regions of the United States. The data I presented shows just that. I divided the data between facilities in the three regions. In order to get a better picture of the care available by region, I used the drilldown Highchart to divide the regions into the types of facilities in each.
