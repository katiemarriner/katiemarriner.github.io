    About Me

    It is fitting that I would discover my love for journalism - I am named after Katie Couric after all. But instead of a traditional journalist, I see myself as part of the "new era" of journalism focused on innovation and technology. I am a graphic designer by nature but a coder by practice and use every opportunity I can to combine my creative and analytical sides. I study visual journalism at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. During the summer of 2013, I interned as a web producer and page designer at the Milwaukee Journal Sentinel. This semester, I am a on staff at Reese News Lab at UNC, where new ideas for the media industry are generated, developed and tested.
