    About Me

    It was written in the stars that I would become a journalist one day - I am named after Katie Couric after all. Although it was a bumpy path before I found my way into the field. I study visual journalism at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. I am a graphic designer by nature but a coder by practice. I am an advocate for "hacker journalism" and hope to become part of the dynamic world of journalism after graduating in May of 2014. During the summer of 2013, I interned as a web producer and page designer at the Milwaukee Journal Sentinel. This year I serve as treasurer for the newly established chapter of the Online News Association at UNC and hone my skills as a designer by creating graphics for the Carolina Union Marketing and Design department.
